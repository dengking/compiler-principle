2.1 Introduction#

For compiler, what can context-free grammar do?

  • Specify syntax as described in Section 2.2
  • Help guide the translation of programs, a grammar-oriented compiling technique known as syntax-directed translation introduced in Section 2.4

Parsing or syntax analysis is introduced in Section 2.4.
